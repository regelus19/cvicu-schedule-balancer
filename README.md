# CVICU Schedule Balancer v30

Standalone self-scheduling balance tool for CVICU nursing staff at The Heart Hospital Baylor Denton.

## 🔗 Live App
**[Open CVICU Schedule Balancer →](https://regelus19.github.io/cvicu-schedule-balancer)**

## What It Does
Uploads a MyTime (UKG/Kronos) schedule export and automatically fills coverage gaps for both Day and Night shifts — respecting all CVICU scheduling rules, staff type requirements, weekend obligations, and shift separation.

## Features
- ☀️ Day shift balancing (7A + MID shifts)
- 🌙 Night shift balancing (7P shifts)
- 📊 Separate Day and Night coverage summary sheets
- 🎚️ Adjustable RN/PCT minimums and per-day caps
- 🔒 ANM-protected (Cruz, Rodriguez, Bruce never moved)
- 📅 Weekday/weekend split requirements
- 📥 Excel output with 5 sheets
- 💬 Built-in feedback form → Rowena.Cruz@UTSouthwestern.edu

## Privacy
All data stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.
